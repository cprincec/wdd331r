Zen Pattern Library

Theme: Infographic

This version of the Zen Garden project is more of an Infographic. It represents an informative web page that utilizes attractive cool colors and contents structured in a way to indicate hierarchy of content and establish sequence of the presented information. This sequence is indicated through the use of line symols linking one informative content piece to to another. This pattern is also embelished by the arrangement of content to wrap in reverse order as shown in the wireframe.

The colors and font chosen are more of decorative and attractive to make for a visually appealing web page and to encourage consumption (reading) of the content.
